Our Mission

​ We want to help teens battle mental illness by teaching them how to care for themselves daily.

​ Self care helps the mind relax and detox after a long and stressful day. Having just 5 minutes a day to yourself and to spend time doing things you love truly make a long-term impact on how you feel. The TAKE5 app helps teenagers focus more on themselves and doing things they love by establishing a simple routine mechanism to track their progress and feelings. All it takes is only 5 minutes out of your day.
